Ontology Mapping back to ToC

Proposed mappings to the Music Ontology and FRBRoo, both of which are based on the FRBR model, are given in the tables below. The mappings enable exploiting the expressivity of the Music Ontology for domain-specific music-related information, as well as of FRBRoo to capture the semantics of bibliographic information and facilitate the integration, mediation, and interchange of bibliographic and museum information.
