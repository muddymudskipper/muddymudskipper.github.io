Introduction back to ToC

The Live Music Event Ontology is designed for the description and linking of live music artifacts. In this data model, physical and digital artifacts and recordings are treated as forms of cultural heritage which can be aligned and distributed along the event timeline. Mappings to existing conceptual models, such as the Music Ontology and FRBRoo, are also described in this document.
